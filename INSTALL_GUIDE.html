Install Guide · Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

Get COMPOUND
on your phone.

A 10-minute, no-coding path to a real installable app on your home screen. Free, permanent, and yours. Do it once — then start the daily loop.

1

Get the file

Download COMPOUND.html (the download card is in our chat). It's a single self-contained file — the whole app in one. Save it somewhere you can find it, like your computer's Desktop or Downloads.

2

Host it free on Netlify Drop

On a computer, go to app.netlify.com/drop. Drag the COMPOUND.html file onto the page. No account needed to start. In a few seconds it gives you a permanent web address (URL) like silver-otter-1234.netlify.app/COMPOUND.html

Copy that URL. (Tip: make a free Netlify account so the link stays live forever and you can drag-drop updated versions later.)

3

Open it on your S24 Ultra

Send yourself the URL however you like (text it to yourself, email, etc.). Open it in Chrome on your phone. The app loads full-screen.

4

Install to your home screen

In Chrome, tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) →

⋮ Menu › Add to Home screen › Install

Chrome will offer "Install app" — choose that (not just "shortcut"). COMPOUND lands in your app drawer with its own icon and opens like a native app, no browser bar. Done

Two honest heads-ups

AI off for now  Food-photo calorie estimates, Quick-Log, "Ask COMPOUND" chat, AI workout generation and AI insights need a real AI service wired in — they'll be quiet on your phone until we build that. Everything else works: rings, to-do loop, check-ins, manual workouts & logging, nutrition tracking, reports, badges.

Good news  Your S24 Ultra is the best-case device. Once installed (step 4), Android keeps your data safe — it won't get wiped like iPhones do after a week unused. It also works offline after the first load.

What "your data lives here" means

  • Everything you log is stored on this one phone. No cloud, no sync to other devices (yet).
  • Don't "clear browsing data" for the installed app — that would erase your history. Normal use is totally safe.
  • When we build the real backend, your daily logging carries forward — nothing you do now is wasted.
