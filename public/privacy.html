Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026 · Operated by Focus Industrial (Australia)

What COMPOUND is

COMPOUND is a personal health and training application. It helps users track workouts, nutrition, body measurements, daily steps, sleep, alcohol intake and daily check-ins.

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address and a password (stored as a secure hash by our authentication provider).
  • Health & activity data you enter: workouts, meals, drinks, weigh-ins, body measurements, steps, walks/runs, sleep, and check-in answers.
  • Connected services (optional): if you connect a device platform (e.g. Garmin), we receive the activity and daily summary data you authorise (such as steps, walks and runs) solely to display it in your account. You can disconnect at any time.

How your data is used

  • To show your own history, trends, streaks and progress inside the app.
  • Meal descriptions and photos you submit for calorie estimation are processed by an AI service (Anthropic) to generate the estimate; they are used for that purpose only.
  • To send you the reminders you switch on (e.g. check-in, weigh-in, workout notifications).

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • Your data is visible only to your own account and to the app's administrator for support purposes.

Where it's stored

Data is stored with Supabase (our database and authentication provider) and protected by row-level security so each account can only access its own records. The app is hosted on Vercel.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete all of your cloud data yourself at any time in Settings → Danger Zone → Clear all cloud data (password confirmation required), or ask us to remove your account entirely.
